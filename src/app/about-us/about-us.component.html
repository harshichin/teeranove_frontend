  • Mission

    Our Mission at Terranova Eco Management is to protect the environment and promote sustainable living by responsibly managing and recycling electronic waste. We Shall leverage cutting-edge technology and synchronized processes to provide comprehensive waste management solutions that are not only cost-effective but also environmentally sustainable. Our commitment to intellectual property integrity and compliance with legal regulations will ensure that we deliver high-quality services that meet the needs of our clients We shall strive to provide safe solutions for disposal of e-waste, while creating economic opportunities and promoting digital inclusion. We are committed to reducing the carbon footprint of electronic waste , preserving natural resources, minimise waste generation and protect our planet for future generations.

  • Vision

    Our Vision at Terranova Eco Management private limited is to be a Global leader in Electronic Waste Management, ITAD and metal recovery. We wish to be recognized for our commitment towards responsible, sustainable and Industry Best Practices. We strive to create a world where electronic waste is no longer a threat to the environment, but instead an opportunity for growth and development. By continuously innovating and improving our processes, we aim to be a role model for the industry fostering a culture of sustainability and responsibility. Together we will work towards a greener future for all.
